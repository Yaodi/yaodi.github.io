About Me

I recently graduated from University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign with a degree in Economics and a minor in Business. During my senior year, I discovered coding was the perfect outlet for my passion of constantly improving and challenging myself. I also discovered that there is a lot of room for creativity in coding, given the amount of different tools and approaches you can use on a single problem. After graduation I began teaching myself to code, and was accepted into Fullstack Academy, an elite coding boot camp where I specialized in developing full-stack JavaScript applications using React, Node, Express, and PostgreSQL.


At Fullstack Academy I gained valuable experience developing applications in team settings following an agile workflow. I am particularly proud capstone project where I led multiple daily scrums while conducting a 2 week agile sprint. During the planning phase, our group conducted a wireframe exercise to ensure that all group members were on the same page with what our minimum viable product was, as well as additional features ordered by priority. This project was a game designed to help people practice their coding skills by writing functions according to instructions. I spearheaded three core aspects of the game, including the code editor integration, function creation/testing, and multiplayer WebSockets functionality. I am not only a skilled programmer but an excellent team player that makes sure my teams members are communicating properly.


Some fun facts about me - I was a competitive gymnast for 10 years during my childhood, I love to travel, try as much new food as possible, and my favorite TV Show of all time is Community!
